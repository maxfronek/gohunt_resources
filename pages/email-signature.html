How to use this tool

(Note: these instructions are for a computer, not a phone or tablet)

  1. Click the copy button, then click the link for your Gmail settings
  2. Scroll down to the Signature section, click "Create New", and name the signature whatever you want
  3. Paste the signature into the text field on the right
  4. Change your name, title, phone number, and email
  5. The GOHUNT address is optional, delete it if you do not want it in your signature
  6. Select your new signature as the default for new messages, and check the box next to "Insert signature before quoted text in replies and remove the "--" line that precedes it."
  7. Scroll to the bottom of the settings page and click "Save Changes"
  8. Your signature will now be added to new emails you compose

Notes on Mobile Applications

Depending on your email use, you may want to set up your signature on your mobile device as well.

Gmail App for iPhone

If you use the Gmail app on an iPhone, make sure that "Use mobile signature" is not checked in the settings. This will automatically use your default desktop signature when sending mobile emails. Note that you will not see the signature while you are drafting your messages on mobile.

Default Mail App for iPhone

You can visit this page on your phone, and copy the signature. Navigate into your phone's settings, select "Email", then select "Signatures". Paste in the content and change the required information as specified above. iPhone signatures are stored as plain text, and will not include the GOHUNT logo.
